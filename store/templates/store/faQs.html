Frequently Asked Questions On TeVez

Q1. I ordered a wrong size, can I exchange for a different size?

A. We are happy to do an exchange and send you the right size.

Q2. How long would it take for my order to arrive?

A. All orders, are shipped 4-5 vusiness days. Although our team will confirm they've recieved your order first.

Q3. Where are TEVEZ products manufactured?

A. Most of our products are manufactured in, Europe, Nigeria, Italy and Korea.

Q4. How can i track my order?

A. Once your order has been dispatched, you will be notified by our delivery service, you can also track your order on our website.

Q5. What are your operating hours?

A. Our hours of operation have been changed from 9:00am-8:00pm to 5:00am-10:00pm.

Q6. Is TEVEZ strictly for women?

A. Yes, although we do have male sales on special occasions like (Father's day). TEVEZ strictly deals with women clothing

Q7. Do you stock Children's clothing as well?

A. At the moment, we only stock clothing for teenagers and adults. Within this range, we carry extra-small to extra-large sizes

Q8. What if there is a mistake with my order?

A. Do contact us as soon as you recieve your order and we will rectify the mistake as soon as possible

Q9. How can i get items in bulk?

A. By simply adding your desired amount to cart, you can get your items in bulk.

Q10. What are the delivery charges?

A. You can see the shipping fees on the checkout process before payment is made.

Q11. Do you have products in stock?

A. All products shown on our site are available in stock.

Q12. Is VAT charged?

A. If you have VAT number, then VAT is not charged.
